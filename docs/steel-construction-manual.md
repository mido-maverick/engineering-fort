# Steel Construction Manual

## Beam Forms

- Simply Supported
- Cantilever
- Continuous

## Beam Formulas

### Simply Supported Beam

Uniformly Distributed Load

$$ V_{max} = \frac{wl}{2} $$
$$ M_{max} = \frac{wl^2}{8} $$
$$ \delta_{max} = \frac{5wl^4}{384EI} $$

### Cantilever Beam

Uniformly Distributed Load

$$ V_{max} = wl $$
$$ M_{max} = \frac{wl^2}{2} $$
$$ \delta_{max} = \frac{wl^4}{8EI} $$

### Continuous Beam

Three Equal Spans - All Spans Loaded

$$ M_{max} = \frac{wl^2}{10} $$
$$ \delta_{max} = \frac{wl^4}{145EI} $$
